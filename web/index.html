About Us


Welcome to MediPet, where we turn medication time into fun time for kids. Our dedicated team from the University of Waterloo created this innovative app to help families manage health routines effortlessly. At MediPet, we're making healthcare a child-friendly, rewarding experience. Join our community and start a new, joyful health journey.

The objective of MediPet is to design, develop, and implement an application to address the critical issue of medication adherence in pediatric patients by introducing a gamified approach to the often challenging task of taking prescribed medication. Tailored for parents of young patients, the app not only serves as a reliable reminder tool but also incorporates a video game/electronic pet feature to make the experience enjoyable for children. Recognizing the significant impact of children's acceptance on medication adherence, our solution focuses on positive reinforcement through play, aligning with recommended strategies. This approach aims to not only encourage proper medication procedures and safety but also foster family bonds and happiness through interactive and rewarding experiences, setting it apart from existing solutions that predominantly target parents and doctors.
